My name is Ed Poole, and I am an undergraduate Web Designer and Developer from Portsmouth, UK. I am orginally from an artistic background, having studied the fine arts all though my school years. I fell in love with the web around 2008, when I realised I could use HTML and CSS to bring my designs to life. Since then I have become obsessed with using the web as a canvas for my work.

My interests surround interface and user experience design. Design is not just about making something look visually appealing; it is about anticipating the decisions a user will make whilst interacting with your system. By fundamentally understanding how someone will react to your design decisions, it allows you to predict and influence their behaviour. This allows us, as designers, to create memorable experiences, which are instinctive to the user.

Projects

Just Delete Me ( justdelete.me ) - This is an open source project jointly created with my friend, Robb Lewis. It lists the direct account deletion links to over 300 diffferent web services. Since it's launch in August 2013, we have had over 3m page views, and we are maintaining around 130k unique visitors a month. Just delete Me has been featured in Wired, Mashable, Techcrunch, The Huffingdon Post, The Wall Street Journal, and many other sites and blogs.
